About Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series...

"Jujutsu Kaisen" is a popular Japanese manga and anime series created by Gege Akutami. The manga began serialization in the "Weekly Shonen Jump" magazine in March 2018, and the anime adaptation premiered in October 2020.

Plot: The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student with exceptional physical abilities. Yuji's life takes a turn when he ingests a powerful cursed object, unleashing a malevolent curse upon the world. To contain the curse, he decides to join the Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical High School, where he learns to control and exorcise curses. Alongside his friends and mentors, he embarks on dangerous missions to save lives and prevent the spread of curses
